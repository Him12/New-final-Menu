Our Story

Founded in 1995, The Leela has been redefining the art of fine dining for over two decades. Our chefs craft dishes that bring together authentic flavors, global techniques, and timeless presentation.

Inspired by India's rich culinary traditions and modern innovations, we’ve created a dining experience that delights the senses — from our handcrafted menus to our elegant ambience.

Whether you're savoring a classic mocktail or exploring our new fusion selections, every visit to The Leela is designed to be unforgettable.
